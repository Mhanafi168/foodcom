Tuna fish

Tuna is a type of healthy fish that contains high levels of protien, fatty acids and omega-3 acids

Benefits of tuna

  • Helps improve heart health
  • It contains 29 grams of protein per 100 grams
  • It contains omega-3 fatty acids that help strengthen memory
  • it contributes to reducing inflammationin the body

Calories in tuna

they vary in calories depending on their type

  • fresh tuna is about 130 calories
  • canned tuna in water is about 100 calories
  • canned tuna in oil is about 190 calories
